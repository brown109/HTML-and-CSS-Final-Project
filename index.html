Feel free to "Contact the Author" if you want to know more about our experience. I'm not "selling" anything via this site. It was more like a gift for the family. But if enough people want a hard copy, I'd just need to collect the cost for printing and shipping. The initial run cost me abour $25 per copy.

Back in 2016, I went on a safari with several family members. It truly was an amazing adventure. From the very start, I had planned to write about it.

What started as a simple blog, turned in to the book you see here. It's a fun read with great stories but even greater photography, provided by my older brother, Alan.

This site features just a few pictures from the several thousand that were taken.
