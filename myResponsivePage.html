The phrase, "The Big Five" can mean many things, but when it comes to animals, it refers to five specific species found in Africa. Of course Africa has thousands of species of animals, but these 5 were considered to be the most elusive and challenging for hunters. I personally can't imagine why anyone would hunt these magnificent creatures for sport, but in a way, my family "hunted" them a few years ago.

By hunt, I mean we went on a safari in hopes of grabbing pictures of them in their natural habitat. What a trip. This site provides a very small glimpse in to what we got to see.
