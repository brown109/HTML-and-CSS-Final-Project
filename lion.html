On the first day of safari, we did catch a glimpse of a lioness and her cubs in the distance.
That night we asked our guide if we might get a closer look. That next morning, we were only a few miles from camp when we saw two males walking towards us. We just sat in awe as they came up right beside the jeep and walked right by. We could have touched them if we were dumb enough to stick our arms out.
