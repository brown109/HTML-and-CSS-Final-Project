We "herd" that a group of elephants was close by

At one point, we were on the opposite side of a stream when we heard that our other vehicle had spotted a baby elephant. My sister almost stole the truck from our driver who was eating lunch. She got him in gear with just a look that said, get over there now or it may be your last lunch! This next video is a different group we spotted later.
